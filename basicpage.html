Editing

Editing is "The Invisible Art". Editors organize footage and meet with the client to understand the total concept of the overall vision for the project and inform the client of the editing techniques that may improve the overall project. The process of joining together the various shots into scenes, and the scenes into a finished movie. The Editor tells a compelling story through hours of looking through footage and then assembling a film, while working quickly to meet the deadlines.
